Hans Zimmer



Hans Zimmer is a German composer who has created some of Hollywood's most spectacular music. Hans has won a Golden Globe, a Tony, an American Music Award, and two Grammy’s for the production of music for movies such as Gladiator, The Lion King, Rain Main, The Last Samurai, The Preachers Wife, Thin Red Line, As Good as It Gets and The Prince of Egypt. Each of those movies he was nominated for an award including the Lion King which the soundtrack even sold over 15 million copies.
