Mark Twain

Mark Twain, or his real name Samuel Langhorne Clemens, is an American Author who grew up in Hannibal, Missouri. Originally born in Florida, Missouri in 1835. He was an amazing author and public speaker His most famous books are the Adventures of Huckleberry Finn wrote in 1855 and The Adventures of Tom Sawyer wrote in 1876. Both these books setting was in his home town Hannibal, Missouri. William Faulkner even said Mark Twain was “the father of American literacture”
